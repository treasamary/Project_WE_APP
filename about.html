ABOUT US


The tribal settlements of Attapady are one of the primitive ones and they still remain isolated fromthemoderndevelopmentprocess. Health,nutritionandeducationproblemsoftribesare numerous as they are the most backward group. Therefore, we intend to solve all the above mentioned problems in Pudur Panchayat of attapady by our application. The problems with health care can be solved with the help of a group of doctors and health workers providing them with all necessary medicines. A balanced diet system initially with the help of nonration beneﬁciaries will be introduced to overcome malnutrition. Educational facilities will beprovidedonline,teaching them academics and personal rights as they are the most haunted ones of corruption. To start with proper orientation will be given and they will be provided with smart phones. The services and products provided will be completely free. From this project, we hope to overcome the problems faced by our fellow humans.
